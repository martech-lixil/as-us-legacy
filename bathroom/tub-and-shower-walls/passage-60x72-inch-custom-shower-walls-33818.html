FEATURES

Passage 60x72" Custom Shower Walls

Give your bathroom a fresh update with the Passage Alcove Shower Wall system from American Standard. Decorative wall panels in today’s most popular patterns are the perfect alternative to tile and grout. Easy to install, these durable, pre-cut panels adhere securely to wall-board and wipe clean with ease.  

 

Features:

  • Stylish patterns instantly elevate your bathroom style
  • 4-piece Glue-Up Installation
  • Pre-cut for quick and easy installation; can easily be trimmed if walls are not perfectly plumb
  • Fits a standard 60"" W x 32"" D x 72"" H alcove
  • High-quality laminate adheres to water-resistant wall board with waterproof adhesive
  • Easy to clean surface
  • Includes 2 side panels measuring 31-7/16 in. x 72 in., and a 2-piece back panel with finished measurement of 59 in. x 72 in.
  • Bull-nosed edge for a custom finish
  • Tongue and groove back panels for a clean installation
  • Create a complete makeover with coordinating Passage shower base, frameless shower door and shower shelves
  • Shop the Passage Shower Collection

 

Wall Set Includes:

  • Four panels of 7/16" (11.5mm) thickness
  • Two back wall panels: 29-1/2" w x 72" h (749 x 1829mm)
  • Two side wall panels: 31-7/16" w x 72" h (787 x 1829mm)
